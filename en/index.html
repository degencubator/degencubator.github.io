Our principles

01

Strategic approach

Digital assets present attractive investment opportunities, but navigating the dynamic environment can be challenging. High volatility, market uncertainty, and the challenge of timing entry and exit points discourage many investors from getting started.

Dopamine Investments’ fully regulated fund provides a structured and diversified approach to this segment. We combine yield-driven, dynamic strategies with low-risk and market-neutral positions to reduce volatility and market risk exposure. Through active management and regular portfolio rebalancing, investors gain optimized exposure without needing to time the market, offering the potential for above-average returns over time.

02

Security

Our core priority is the maximum protection of client assets. The fund applies a comprehensive security framework, focusing on digital infrastructure and cybersecurity.

We partner with BitGo, Inc., a regulated and licensed custodian specializing in digital asset security. BitGo operates across the United States, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia, and has been active in the sector since 2013. The firm safeguards over $100 billion in assets and meets institutional compliance, reliability, and risk management standards.

03

Transparency

Dopamine Investments is structured to provide a high level of transparency and oversight. Governance is based on clearly defined processes supported by independent supervision and regular reporting.

Oversight includes the appointment of both an independent administrator and an independent director. Our fund is audited annually and operates under the supervision of its regulatory authority. It is licensed under Section 4(3) of the Cayman Islands Mutual Funds Act (Revised).

The Cayman Islands is a globally recognized financial center, particularly for U.S.-linked investment structures. Its legal framework is based on British common law and is widely regarded as one of the most stable and investor-friendly jurisdictions for investment funds and financial institutions. The region also offers long-term geopolitical and legal stability, which is an important consideration for investors seeking broader geographic diversification.
